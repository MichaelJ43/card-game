# Canasta (draw-two practice)

**Not** full canasta: this is a two-player **shedding drill** on a 108-card shoe (two standard decks plus four jokers) to practice draw/discard tempo.

## Goal

1. On your turn, **draw two** cards from stock (or fewer if the stock is almost empty), then **discard exactly one** card face up.
2. The first player to **empty their hand** after a discard wins the round and earns the match point.
3. Default match is first to **3** round wins (override via house rules when the manifest enables a match).

## Deal

1. Eleven cards each, then one face-up starter discard; the rest is stock.

## Turns

1. If the stock still has cards, you must **Draw two** before discarding.
2. If the stock is **empty**, you skip the draw step and discard from your current hand.
3. End your turn after the discard unless you went out.

## Notes

1. There are no melds, piles, frozen discards, or canasta bonuses in this module.
2. Jokers are ordinary distinct cards here (they still draw and discard like anything else).
